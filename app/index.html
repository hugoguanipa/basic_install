We are a catalyst fund accelerating the reversal of climate change through intelligence, funding, and diffusion of innovations in natural capital.

Blue Dot Project is a for-impact organization aiming to bring economic, social, and natural systems back into equilibrium. We support carefully vetted non-governmental organizations (NGOs), startups, and initiatives that protect and enhance our planet’s natural capital through a catalyst fund. Natural capital includes all the resources on Earth that both support human life and make it worth living. It includes water, minerals, oil, vegetation, animals and marine life, soil, and air.
